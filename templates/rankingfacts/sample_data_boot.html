CSRankings is a metrics-based ranking of top computer science institutions around the world, which include approximately 50 universities and 5 variables, such as faculty size, count of publications, and averaged GRE score.

The “German Credit” dataset hosted on (UCI’s Machine Learning Repository) contains approximately 1000 observations, with 20 variables. The original dependent variable is whether or not an “individual” has good credit record.

ProPublica COMPAS includes approximately sampled 1000 users' recidivism information, containing criminal history, jail and prison time, demographics and COMPAS risk scores for defendants from Broward County.
